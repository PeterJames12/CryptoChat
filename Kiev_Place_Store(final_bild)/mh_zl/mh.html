Sviatopolk Izjaslovich (Yaroslav's Mudriy grandson) built in 1108-1113 St. Michael's Golden-Domed Monastery.

St. Michael's Golden-Domed Monastery is cross-dome tample with three naves and one gilt dome, it was built by kyiv king Sviatopolk Izjaslovich.

Monastery was built on precipice of Starokyivski mountain, above ancient Borichev's slope (slope to river Dnipro).One of the reasons, why was built Monastery, was Sviatopolk's victory over Polovtsians.Monastery was buil of stone and brick on lime-cement mortar.Walls decorated by unique mosaics and murals, that brought for it great glory.

After 60 years by Kyiv goverment and town community St. Michael's Golden-Domed Monastery was restored in all his previou beauty. During Monastery recreate were returned murals and mosaics.

Address: Kyiv, Triokhsviatytelska St. 8, Metro station Poshtova Ploshcha.
