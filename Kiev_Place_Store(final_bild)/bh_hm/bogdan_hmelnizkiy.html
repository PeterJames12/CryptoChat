Statue of Bohdan Khmelnytsky

Statue of Bohdan Khmelnytsky is peculiar symbol of capital. It placed in honor of ukrainian hetman Bohdan Khmelnytsky, that knew in ukrainian history as remarkable commander and state figure.

It solemnly opened in Kyiv in 11 July 1888.

Idea to placed monument of hetman has already been in 1850s . Organizer of creating statue was chief of archaeological commission Michael Yuzefovych. In 1869 a decree of placed monument was issued, in a year was allowed to collect money on it. Was collected some money,because it endowed only poor people: villagers, warriors and artisans.

Statue was placed on Sophia Square. First idea was to turn it towards Warsaw in order to Bohdan Khmelnytsky threaten by mace to it, but mace show in towards to Moskau,that invite to peace .

Address: Sophia Square.
